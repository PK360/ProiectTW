My interests

1. Portal 1 (Valve, 2007) / Portal 2 (Valve, 2011)

AHHHH I love this game series so much it's so near and dear to my heart....Portal 1 was one of the first games I purchased and back then it was really revolutionary for me: the characters are all funny in a really witty way, the story is really engaging and the puzzle gameplay is really well put together and fun. Overall, a really really REALLY good pair of games that I would genuinely recommend. 10/10

2. Pokémon (game series + anime)

I LOVE POKEMONNNNN!!!!!! It's a really fun RPG series that has a lot of cute and awesome creatures that you need to collect and battle with! The battle mechanics may be a bit sluggish at first, but you'll get used to it pretty quickly. ALSO, BW ARE GOOD GAMES NO MATTER WHAT THE HATERS SAY.

The anime is really cute as well, I love how wacky and silly it can be sometimes, but it also gets kinda serious when it needs to. Also, the animation is REALLY good, especially in the latest shows.

3. DnD (Dungeons & Dragons - 4th Edition)

I love this game so much!!! Getting together with my group of irl friends and starting a campaign that usually lasts for a month or so is super awesome and exciting every time!! I love making new characters and seeing them grow together with our party...

Even though this version of DnD might have its flaws, it was the first version I started playing (ever) so it carries some nostalgic value to it :>
